Brake Repair FAQs

How much does mobile brake repair cost?

Most brake jobs range from $150-$500 depending on your vehicle and whether you need pads, rotors, or additional work. We provide a detailed estimate before starting any repairs, and our mobile service doesn't carry extra fees—you pay the same price as you would at a traditional shop.

How long does a brake job take?

A standard brake pad replacement typically takes 1-2 hours. If rotors need resurfacing or replacement, allow 2-3 hours. Complex jobs involving calipers or brake lines may take longer. We'll give you an accurate time estimate when we assess your vehicle.

Do I need to replace all four brakes at once?

Not necessarily. Front brakes wear faster than rear brakes due to weight transfer during braking. We assess each axle independently and only recommend replacement when needed. However, we always replace pads in pairs (both fronts or both rears) to maintain even braking performance.

Is mobile brake service as good as a shop?

Absolutely. We use the same professional-grade tools, diagnostic equipment, and quality parts as any brick-and-mortar shop. The difference is convenience—we bring the shop to you. Our experienced technicians have the same expertise you'd find at a dealership.
