Why Luxury, Exotic & Supercars Need More Than a General Shop

High-performance and luxury vehicles are engineered to tolerances that general shops aren't equipped to meet. Whether it's the dry-sump oiling system on a Lamborghini V10, the carbon-ceramic brakes on a Ferrari, the adaptive air suspension on a Range Rover, or the CAN-bus network in a modern AMG—these vehicles demand specialized knowledge, tooling, and fluids.

Mobile Mechanic Muscle brings that expertise directly to your location. Whether your McLaren needs a brake service in Brentwood, your Porsche is throwing a suspension fault in Belle Meade, or your Koenigsegg needs pre-event inspection, we handle it on-site with the precision you'd expect from a specialty dealership—without the markup or the hassle.

We work on the vehicles that make general shops hesitate—and the ones they flat-out refuse. That's the point.

What We Handle

Every service executed with the correct tooling, fluids, and specifications for your specific vehicle.

01

Brake Systems

Carbon-ceramic and performance pads, rotors, brake fluid flush with manufacturer-spec DOT ratings, caliper service, and electronic parking brake resets. Experienced with Brembo, CCM, and PCCB systems.

02

Diagnostics & Electronics

Factory-level OBD scans across every module—engine, transmission, ABS, airbag, and body control. Not just the check engine light.

03

Suspension & Air Ride

Air spring replacement, compressor diagnostics, ride-height calibration, and adaptive damper service for Land Rover, Mercedes, BMW, Porsche, McLaren hydraulic systems, and Lamborghini magnetorheological setups.

04

Cooling, Turbo & Supercharger Systems

Coolant flush with OEM-approved fluids, thermostat replacement, turbo boost-leak testing, supercharger service, and intercooler inspection—from twin-turbo V8s to flat-plane crank Ferrari engines.

05

Oil & Fluid Service

Correct weight and specification oil (LL-01, 229.5, A40), transmission fluid, differential service, and power steering flush—manufacturer-approved only.

06

Pre-Purchase & Event Inspection

Considering a used exotic or luxury vehicle? Comprehensive mechanical and electronic inspection at the seller's location before you commit. Also available: pre-track and car-show prep inspections.

The Difference

What Sets Us Apart

The details that matter when your vehicle has more engineering per square inch than a fighter jet.

Correct Tooling

Torx, triple-square, and specialty fasteners are standard on European and exotic vehicles. From Lamborghini center-lock wheel tools to Ferrari timing belt kits—we carry the exact hardware your vehicle requires. No improvising, no stripped bolts.

Make-Specific Scan Tools

Generic code readers miss half the story. We access the full module set on Ferrari, Lamborghini, McLaren, Porsche, BMW, Mercedes, Land Rover, Audi, and Jaguar platforms—not just the engine ECU.

OEM-Spec Parts & Fluids

Wrong coolant, wrong oil spec, or incompatible brake fluid can damage expensive components. We match every fluid and part to the manufacturer's specification. No substitutions.

Complex System Experience

Air suspensions, adaptive headlights, electronic differentials, E-Gear / DCT transmissions, and mid-engine cooling layouts require more than a repair manual. We've worked on these systems hands-on—from Huracáns to Cayennes—and know the common failure points.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Do you work on all luxury, exotic, and supercars?

We specialize in Ferrari, Lamborghini, McLaren, Porsche, Mercedes-AMG, BMW M, Bentley, Maserati, Audi RS, Land Rover, Jaguar, and Volvo. We also service Koenigsegg, Aston Martin, Rolls-Royce, Lotus, and other exotic platforms. If your vehicle isn't listed, call us—we likely have experience with it.

Is mobile service safe for supercars and exotics?

Yes. We use the same factory-level diagnostic tools, OEM-specification parts, and manufacturer-approved fluids that a specialty dealership would use. Whether it's a Ferrari 488 or a Range Rover—the only difference is location. We perform the work where your vehicle is, instead of a shop bay.

How does pricing compare to a dealership?

Our rates are competitive with independent European specialty shops and typically 30–50% less than dealership service departments. You also avoid towing fees and loaner car charges. Written estimate before any work begins.

Can you handle warranty or recall work?

Warranty and manufacturer recall work must be performed by an authorized dealership. For out-of-warranty vehicles, we perform the same repairs at a lower cost using OEM-equivalent or genuine parts.
