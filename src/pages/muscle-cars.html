Muscle Car Service FAQ

Do you work on classic muscle cars from the 60s and 70s?

Yes. We service classic Camaros, Mustangs, Challengers, Chevelles, GTOs, 'Cudas, and other vintage American muscle. We're comfortable with carburetors, points ignition, drum brakes, and everything else that comes with these platforms.

Can you work on modern Hellcats, GT500s, and ZL1s?

Absolutely. We have factory-level diagnostic tools for late-model HEMI, Coyote, and LT-powered vehicles. Oil changes, brake service, suspension work, diagnostics, and bolt-on performance installs.

Do you install aftermarket parts?

Yes — intake manifolds, headers, exhaust, cold air intakes, coilovers, brake kits, and other bolt-on modifications. We don't do custom fabrication or engine builds, but we handle everything that bolts on.

What if my muscle car doesn't run?

That's exactly when mobile service makes the most sense. We come to wherever the car sits — your garage, storage unit, or driveway — diagnose the issue, and get it running on-site if possible.
